A STAR WARS RPG THROWDOWN
Jedis vs Siths

INSTRUCTIONS

You ready for the lightsaber swingingest, Sith Lord shockingest, Jedi knight stompingest throwdown ever? You've come to the right place. Here's how this is going to work:
  1. Choose a Jedi to fight first by clicking one of the characters on the left.
  2. The Sith will come at you in order one at a time. Click the "ROLL" button to initate each round of the fight. The results of each round will be displayed in the roll history.
  3. The Sith Lord will attack your Jedi first because, as Yoda taught Luke, "a Jedi uses the force for knowledge and defense, never for attack".
  4. After being attacked, your Jedi will counter-attack the Sith. Health is reduced immediately on attack and counter.
  5. When a character's health has reached 0, they are out of the fight. For Jedi, you'll need to choose the next knight up by clicking another Jedi not already eliminated from the fight.
  6. The goal is to defeat all of the Sith before all of your Jedi have been eliminated from the fight.
  7. HINT: If you know how the Sith were actually defeated, you'll be able to win this game.
